---
title: "Bridge Integration"
description: "Fiat on/off-ramping and card issuing."
---

Remlo partners directly with Bridge to handle all fiat-to-stablecoin conversion operations inside the platform.

Once an employer passes KYB, Remlo spins up a dedicated Bridge virtual account directly coupled to the employer's `PayrollTreasury` address on the Tempo network.
Funds sent via ACH or wire instantly mint as tokenized stablecoin pathUSD inside the treasury upon receipt.

Employees interact with the Bridge ecosystem on the egress side. Once salary is streamed or batched into their Tempo wallets, they can order and attach a virtual Bridge Visa card directly funded by their decentralized wallet balance or seamlessly trigger fiat off-ramping to a local bank account.
