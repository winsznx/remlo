---
title: "Privy Integration"
description: "Wallet authentication and employee onboarding."
---

Remlo utilizes Privy to drastically reduce friction associated with self-custody wallet creation and management across the userbase.

### Employer Context
Employers create their company structures using seamless social links or email login processes. Privy abstracts the complex custodial signing logic underneath the dashboard hood, allowing administrators to push payroll boundaries securely without risking compromised seed phrases.

### Employee Context
New employees invited into the Remlo ecosystem do not need to install browser extensions or rely on hardware wallets to receive their payroll. Claiming their profile and finishing KYC through the employee portal provisions an embedded software wallet through Privy securely tied to their credentials.

All signatures involving streaming claims or off-ramping executes natively within the embedded footprint.
